4. Teacher notes：哪些適合正式作文？

source-backed route: Unit 11 / Unit 61 register judgment: assistant teaching note

表達正式作文使用感更穩替代
on the one hand / on the other hand可用，但不要一篇文章一直套。One possible objection is... / Another way to view this is...
be that as it may有書面感，適合承認後轉回主張。Even so / Nevertheless / This does not mean that...
to put it in a nutshell偏口語、教學可用；正式考場可保守一點。In brief / In short / Taken together
common ground很好用，適合討論雙方可接受的基礎。shared basis / point of agreement
stand your ground形象鮮明，較適合議論或口語化評論。maintain one's position / remain firm

5. Micro-practice：把普通句改成有 stance

普通句改寫方向來源路線

There are good things and bad things about social media.On the one hand, social media connects students; on the other hand, it can easily distract them.Unit 11 p.29
Both sides agree on one thing.The debate should begin from this common ground: students need both safety and concentration.Unit 61 p.129
