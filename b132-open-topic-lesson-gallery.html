How to use this with students

  • Pick one topic and open its lesson pack.
  • Have students read the four quick fragments first.
  • Use the 40-card evidence library only when they need stronger examples or a second position.
  • Turn one source fragment into a speaking answer, then into a short essay paragraph.

What changed

These are not blank prompts. Each topic now begins with actual public-domain source fragments, original-source links, and a clear route from reading to speaking to writing.
