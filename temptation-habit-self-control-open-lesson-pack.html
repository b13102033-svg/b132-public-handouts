Speaking ladder

Step 1 · Define the pressure.

Describe one temptation people often underestimate. Use one source fragment as your opening reference.

Step 2 · Draw a line.

When is “I couldn’t help it” understandable, and when is it only an excuse?

Step 3 · Upgrade the answer.

Move from personal opinion to principle: habit, responsibility, character, environment, and repeated choice.

Writing prompt

Some people believe self-control is mainly a matter of personal willpower. Others argue that habits and environment shape our choices more deeply than we realize.
