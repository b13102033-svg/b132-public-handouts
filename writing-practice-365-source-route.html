Best uses

Daily writing habit

Short guided Q&A prompts that help students generate a manageable English answer without freezing.

Use when a student says: I have ideas, but I do not know how to start in English.

Speaking-to-writing bridge

The book is useful for turning simple oral answers into short written responses with a clearer stance.

Use for GEPT/GSAT warm-ups: answer orally first, then refine into a paragraph.

Topic bank

Its everyday themes give quick classroom prompts: online opinions, rumors, role models, plans, dreams, family, belief, opportunity.

Use when a lesson needs concrete personal-context topics rather than abstract essay titles.
