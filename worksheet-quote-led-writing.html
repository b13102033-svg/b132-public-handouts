Turn quote into claim

This quote is not mainly about ___; it is really about ___.

Explain, do not decorate

The quote matters because it changes how we see ___.

Quote integration moves

  • This line suggests that...
  • What makes the quotation useful is not its wording alone, but its idea that...
  • The quotation becomes persuasive when we connect it to...
  • A weaker essay would merely repeat the quote; a stronger essay uses it to define a problem.
