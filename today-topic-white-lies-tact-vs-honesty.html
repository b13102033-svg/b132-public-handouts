2. 今天的核心問題

Question: Is it ever better to tell a white lie than to be completely honest?

Position A: strict honestyComplete honesty builds trust, even when the truth is uncomfortable.
Position B: tactful kindnessA small white lie may be kinder when the truth would only hurt someone without helping them.
Position C: dangerous comfortWhite lies can become a habit of avoiding difficult but necessary conversations.
C1 balanceThe real issue is not honesty versus lying, but whether the speaker is protecting the other person or protecting themselves from discomfort.

3. 口說素材：從 B2 到 C1

功能B2 可用句C1 升級
表態I think white lies can sometimes be acceptable.I would not defend lying in general, but I do think tact matters in emotionally delicate situations.
舉例For example, if a friend asks about a bad haircut, I may not tell the whole truth.If the truth serves no practical purpose and only humiliates the listener, a tactful response may be more humane.
讓步However, people should not lie about serious things.That said, once a white lie hides information someone has a right to know, it stops being kindness and becomes manipulation.
總結So I think it depends on the situation.So the boundary depends on intention, consequence, and whether the truth would genuinely help the other person.

60 秒口說骨架

Position: I think white lies are sometimes acceptable, but only in small social situations. Reason: Complete honesty is not always the same as kindness. Example: If a friend is already nervous about a presentation, I might encourage them first instead of pointing out every weakness immediately. Concession: However, if the lie affects an important decision, honesty is necessary. Conclusion: The best answer is not brutal honesty or careless lying, but tactful honesty.

4. 寫作素材：Task 2 / 英檢申論

Writing prompt: Some people believe it is always better to be completely honest, while others think small white lies can be acceptable. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

段落論點可用語言
Intro界定爭點The debate is not simply about truth and falsehood, but about how language affects trust, dignity, and social relationships.
Body 1支持誠實Honesty is essential when people need accurate information to make decisions.
Body 2支持委婉In minor social situations, tact can prevent unnecessary embarrassment.
Body 3界線A white lie is defensible only when it protects someone’s feelings without taking away their right to know the truth.

示範段落

In my view, white lies can be acceptable only when they are used with tact and do not hide information that another person has a right to know. Complete honesty is valuable because it builds trust, but honesty can also become unnecessarily cruel if it is delivered without sensitivity. For instance, telling a nervous friend every weakness in their presentation five minutes before they speak may be honest, but it is not helpful. A more tactful response would encourage them first and offer specific advice later. However, if the truth affects a serious decision, a white lie no longer holds water as an act of kindness.

5. 互動練習

  1. 口說 20 秒：只回答 yes/no + one reason。
  2. 口說 60 秒：加入 example + concession。
  3. 改寫：把 “Lying is bad” 改成一個有灰色地帶的 C1 thesis。
  4. 詞彙：用 tactful / brutally honest / spare someone’s feelings / hold water 各造一句。
  5. 辯論：哪一種情境不該 white lie？health, money, relationship, performance feedback。
