今日目標

學生下課時要能做到：

  • 口說：先答、再解釋、再舉例、再讓步。
  • 寫作：有清楚 claim、有連接、有反方處理。
  • 用字：不只 good/bad/think，而能用 stance 與 argument 語言。

0. 教師開場：考什麼，今天練什麼

GEPT 中高級複試的課堂重點可切成兩條線：寫作要處理中譯英與引導寫作；口說要處理回答問題、看圖敘述、申述。B2→C1 的差別不只是少犯錯，而是回答要更有組織、語氣更成熟、論點更能站得住。

1. 10 分鐘診斷

Speaking diagnostic

Question: Some people say students should spend less time memorizing vocabulary and more time using English in real situations. Do you agree?

15-second answer:

Student speaks. Teacher notes: directness / reason / example / connector.

Writing diagnostic

Write 80 words: Is learning English through exams useful?

Student writes here. Teacher marks only three things: claim, connection, example.

Teacher rule: do not correct everything. First identify whether the student has a claim problem, a development problem, or a wording/register problem.

2. 核心招式一：先讓步，再表態

很多 B2 學生只會說 I agree 或 I disagree。中高級複試要更像真實討論：你可以先承認對方有道理，再說你的保留。

Safe speakingI see your point, but I am not fully convinced.
More formalWhile this view has some merit, I still have some reservations.
Writing modeAlthough this argument is reasonable in some cases, it overlooks a more important issue.

Source route

這組來自 B132 stance route 的跨來源整合：Collocations in Use 處理 agree/disagree 搭配；Business Vocabulary in Use 處理會議式保留不同意；Cambridge IELTS 10 處理 speaking opinion / writing personal view。

3. 核心招式二：把理由說成「論點」

B2 常見句子：I think it is bad because it is not good. 這不是錯，而是太薄。中高級要能說一個論點是否有支撐、是否有說服力。

WeakThis idea is bad.
BetterThis argument is not very convincing because it does not explain how students would actually benefit.
StrongerThe argument does not fully hold water: it assumes that more practice automatically leads to better communication.
Positive versionA more solid argument would connect exam preparation with real communicative tasks.

Source route

用 weak-argument route 教「論點強弱階梯」：not hold water / solid argument / logical argument / convincing / persuasive / pick holes in。

論點站不住腳 route not hold water solid argument convincing persuasive

4. 核心招式三：提出建議，不要只說 should

考試的 solution 題、口說申述題、看圖敘述後的推論，都需要提出想法。不要一直用 I think we should...。

BasicSchools should give students more chances to use English.
BetterOne possible solution is to give students more chances to use English in realistic situations.
AdvancedI would put forward a more balanced approach: exams should support communication, not replace it.

Source route

offer-thoughts route 專門處理 offer a suggestion / provide input / food for thought / put forward an argument or proposal。

提供想法 route put forward offer a suggestion input

5. 核心招式四：用連接詞做出層次

句子要有層次，不是一直用 and / but / because。今天先掌握四個：

功能句型課堂例句原書路線
讓步Although...Although exams can motivate students, they should not be the only goal of learning.200句型 p.130
限定理由in that...Real communication is useful in that it forces students to organize ideas quickly.200句型 p.140
前提判斷given that...Given that many students already know grammar rules, they need more practice developing ideas.200句型 p.144
累加強調not only...but also...A good answer not only gives an opinion but also explains why that opinion matters.200句型 p.150

6. 口說練習：15 秒 → 30 秒 → 90 秒

Prompt: Some people believe that English classes should focus more on speaking than grammar. What do you think?

15 seconds

Direct answer + one reason.

I partly agree because...

30 seconds

Answer + reason + example.

I see why..., but...

90 seconds

Answer + reason + example + concession + final stance.

Although..., I would argue that...
示範答案

I partly agree. Speaking practice is important because it forces students to organize ideas quickly, which is exactly what they need in real communication. However, I would not say grammar is unimportant. A student who speaks fluently but makes serious grammar mistakes may still fail to express precise ideas. A more balanced approach is to teach grammar through speaking tasks, so students can use sentence patterns while discussing real topics.

Source-backed synthesis: stance route + 200 sentence patterns + GEPT speaking rubric ideas. Not copied from a textbook.

7. 寫作練習：150-180 words 引導寫作

Prompt: Some people believe that learning English through exams is effective, while others think real communication matters more. Write 150-180 words to express your view.

段落功能你要寫什麼可用骨架
Claim你的主張，不要兩邊都講到沒有立場。I would argue that .
Reason用 in that / because 指出原因。This is important in that .
Example一個具體學生場景。For example, .
Concession承認另一邊有道理。Although , .
Solution提出平衡做法。A more balanced approach is to .
示範 outline

I would argue that exams are useful, but they should not become the center of English learning. Exams can motivate students and help teachers check progress. However, real communication matters because it forces learners to organize ideas, respond quickly, and choose words for a real purpose. For example, a student may know many grammar rules but still struggle to explain an opinion in a conversation. Although exam practice has value, it should be connected to speaking and writing tasks. A more balanced approach is to use exams as a tool for feedback, not as the final goal of learning.

8. 中譯英精準度練習

中文：許多學生不是不會文法，而是不知道如何把想法組織成清楚、有層次的論點。

Your translation:
建議譯法與拆解

Many students do not necessarily lack grammar knowledge; rather, they do not know how to organize their ideas into a clear, well-developed argument.

  • not necessarily lack... 比 do not have 更精準。
  • rather 做「不是 A，而是 B」的轉向。
  • clear, well-developed argument 對應清楚、有層次的論點。
