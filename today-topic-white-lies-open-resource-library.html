4. 可直接丟給學生的高張力問題

1. Is a white lie still a lie if it protects someone from unnecessary pain? 2. Is brutal honesty more moral, or just less socially intelligent? 3. When does tact become manipulation? 4. Can fiction tell emotional truth by inventing facts? 5. Are people who never lie trustworthy, or simply dangerous in social situations? 6. Is self-deception worse than lying to others?
