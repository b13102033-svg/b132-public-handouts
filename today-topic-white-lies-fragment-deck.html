1. Warm-up / 引爆討論

twain art vs custom lying

On the Decay of the Art of Lying · Mark Twain · provocation · B2-C1

lyingironysocial_arttone

My complaint simply concerns the decay of the art of lying.

Twain 不是單純鼓勵說謊，而是用反諷把「社交謊言」說成一門快失傳的藝術。適合教 irony 和 provocative thesis。

問：Is Twain defending lying, mocking society, or doing both?
輸出句：The issue is not whether people lie, but whether they lie with tact, purpose, and social awareness.

aesop wolf credibility

Aesop’s Fables: The Shepherd’s Boy and the Wolf · Aesop · warm_up · B1-B2

trustcredibilityhabitual_lying

There is no believing a liar, even when he speaks the truth.

最適合當開場：學生都懂，但可升級成 credibility / reputation / trust cost。

問：Why does repeated lying damage future truth?
輸出句：A lie does not only distort one fact; it damages the speaker’s credibility.

2. 核心辯論片段

twain judicious lying

On the Decay of the Art of Lying · Mark Twain · argument_strength · C1

judiciouswhite_lieethics

Judicious lying is what the world needs.

把 lying 加上 judicious，立刻變成灰色倫理題：問題不是 lie vs truth，而是 judgment。

問：Can a lie be morally better because it is judicious? Who decides?
輸出句：A white lie may be defensible when it is judicious, limited, and meant to prevent unnecessary harm.

twain brutal truth

On the Decay of the Art of Lying · Mark Twain · core_debate · B2-C1

brutal_truthinjurytact

An injurious truth has no merit over an injurious lie.

這是今天最強的討論核心：truth 不自動等於 virtue；如果 truth 造成無必要傷害，它也可能失去道德優勢。

問：Do you agree that a harmful truth is no better than a harmful lie?
輸出句：Honesty loses its moral force when it becomes a way to hurt rather than to help.

good manners is the sign

問：Can politeness be sincere, or is it always performance?
輸出句：Politeness is not necessarily fake; it can be a form of respect made visible.

ladies vase false politeness

The Ladies’ Vase: Polite Manual for Young Ladies · Anonymous · contrast_set · B2-C1

false_politenesstactsurface

True politeness is the smoothness of a refined mind and the tact of a kind heart.

這句可當今天的核心定義：tact 是 kind heart 的觸感，不是裝客氣。

問：How is true politeness different from fake politeness?
輸出句：Tact is not the opposite of honesty; it is honesty shaped by kindness.

3. C1 延伸片段

twain silent lie

On the Decay of the Art of Lying · Mark Twain · advanced_nuance · C1

silent_lieomissiondeception

We have the silent lie—the deception which one conveys by simply keeping still.

這張卡把學生帶到 omission / concealment：不說謊話，但透過沉默讓對方誤會，算不算 lying？

問：Is keeping silent morally different from telling a false statement?
輸出句：Omission can become deception when silence is used to create a false impression.

wilde art vs nature

fictionarttruthnature

What Art really reveals to us is Nature’s lack of design.

Wilde 把 fiction / art 放在 truth 之上。這適合引出：小說、修辭、善意謊言是不是都在製造一種「更有用的真實」？

問：Can fiction reveal truth by refusing to be factual?
輸出句：Fiction may be factually false but emotionally or morally revealing.
