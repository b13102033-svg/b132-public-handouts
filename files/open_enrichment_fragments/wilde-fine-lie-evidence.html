What is a fine lie? Simply that which is its own evidence.

教學切入

Wilde 的句子很適合教 paradox：a lie that is its own evidence。學生可以學到高級抽象表達。

討論題：Why would evidence make a lie less artistic in Wilde’s view?
英文輸出：Wilde treats lying not as moral failure but as imaginative performance.
