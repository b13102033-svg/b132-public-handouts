On the Decay of the Art of Lying

Mark Twain · literary_humor_ethics · provocation · B2-C1

lying irony social_art tone

My complaint simply concerns the decay of the art of lying.

Classroom angle

Twain is not simply encouraging lies; he uses irony to present social lying as a lost art. Useful for irony and provocative thesis statements.

Discussion: Is Twain defending lying, mocking society, or doing both?
Sentence model: The issue is not whether people lie, but whether they lie with tact, purpose, and social awareness.
Activity: Ask students to rewrite the claim as a serious IELTS-style thesis.
