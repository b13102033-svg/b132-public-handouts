On the Decay of the Art of Lying

lyingironysocial_arttone

My complaint simply concerns the decay of the art of lying.

教學切入

Twain 不是單純鼓勵說謊，而是用反諷把「社交謊言」說成一門快失傳的藝術。適合教 irony 和 provocative thesis。

討論題：Is Twain defending lying, mocking society, or doing both?
英文輸出：The issue is not whether people lie, but whether they lie with tact, purpose, and social awareness.
活動：Ask students to rewrite the claim as a serious IELTS-style thesis.
