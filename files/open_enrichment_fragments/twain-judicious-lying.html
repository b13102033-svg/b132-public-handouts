On the Decay of the Art of Lying

Mark Twain · rhetoric_ethics · argument_strength · C1

judiciouswhite_lieethics

Judicious lying is what the world needs.

教學切入

把 lying 加上 judicious，立刻變成灰色倫理題：問題不是 lie vs truth，而是 judgment。

討論題：Can a lie be morally better because it is judicious? Who decides?
英文輸出：A white lie may be defensible when it is judicious, limited, and meant to prevent unnecessary harm.
活動：Have students define “judicious” with three conditions.
