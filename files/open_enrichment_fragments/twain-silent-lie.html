We have the silent lie—the deception which one conveys by simply keeping still.

Classroom angle

This card introduces omission and concealment: if you say nothing but let someone misunderstand, is that still lying?

Discussion: Is keeping silent morally different from telling a false statement?
Sentence model: Omission can become deception when silence is used to create a false impression.
Activity: Make students classify cases: lie, tact, omission, manipulation.
