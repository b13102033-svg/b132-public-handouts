On the Decay of the Art of Lying

Mark Twain · pragmatics_omission · advanced_nuance · C1

silent_lieomissiondeception

We have the silent lie—the deception which one conveys by simply keeping still.

教學切入

這張卡把學生帶到 omission / concealment：不說謊話，但透過沉默讓對方誤會，算不算 lying？

討論題：Is keeping silent morally different from telling a false statement?
英文輸出：Omission can become deception when silence is used to create a false impression.
活動：Make students classify cases: lie, tact, omission, manipulation.
