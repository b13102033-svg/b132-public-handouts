Intentions: The Decay of Lying

Oscar Wilde · literature_aesthetics · creative_extension · C1

fictionarttruthnature

What Art really reveals to us is Nature’s lack of design.

教學切入

Wilde 把 fiction / art 放在 truth 之上。這適合引出：小說、修辭、善意謊言是不是都在製造一種「更有用的真實」？

討論題：Can fiction reveal truth by refusing to be factual?
英文輸出：Fiction may be factually false but emotionally or morally revealing.
活動：Students compare “factually true” and “emotionally true”.
