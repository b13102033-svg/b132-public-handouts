There is no believing a liar, even when he speaks the truth.

Classroom angle

Best opening source: students understand it immediately, but it can be upgraded into credibility, reputation, and trust cost.

Discussion: Why does repeated lying damage future truth?
Sentence model: A lie does not only distort one fact; it damages the speaker’s credibility.
Activity: Students turn the fable into a modern social-media example.
