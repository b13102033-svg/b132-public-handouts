Classroom angle

This source connects politeness with respect and sincerity, not surface manners. It can be compared with formulaic social niceness.

Discussion: Can politeness be sincere, or is it always performance?
Sentence model: Politeness is not necessarily fake; it can be a form of respect made visible.
Activity: Students define true politeness in one sentence.
