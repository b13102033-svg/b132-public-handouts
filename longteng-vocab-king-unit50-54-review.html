卷五｜寫作應用卷 3 prompts

每題 80-120 字。請至少使用 8 個 Unit 50-54 單字，並注意搭配自然度。

Prompt 1｜School Rules

Some students believe that strict school rules protect students, while others think they limit freedom. What is your opinion?

Useful words: regulate, conform, violation, compromise, hostile, initiative, justify, beneficial, adolescent, interfere/interference.

Prompt 2｜Cultural Heritage

Your school is planning an exhibition about local culture. Explain why cultural heritage matters and how students can participate.

Useful words: heritage, indigenous, presentation, participant, interpret, perception, highlight, preserve, ceremony, cuisine.

Prompt 3｜Technology and Teenagers

Do smartphones make teenagers more connected or more distracted? Give reasons and examples.

Useful words: adolescent, cognitive, accessible, widespread, trigger, fatigue, preference, modify, excessive, contemporary.
